# Fujifilm X100V

A premium point-and-shoot APS-c format camera. Has a fixed 24mm f/2 lens (35mm full frame
equivalent).

### General features

* APS-c
* Point-and-shoot
* Mostly weather sealed
  * Lens is not, but with an adapter ring and filter, completes weather sealing
* No IBIS
* Flip out screen
