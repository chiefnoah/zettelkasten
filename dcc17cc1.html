Desk Plants

Plants that can survive well in a desk/office setting. This includes only needing occasional watering, indirect or little sunlight, and relatively small size.

Snake Plants

Dracaena Trifasciata

Water: 1-2x per month

Light: part (direct) sun, part shade

Money Plant

Epipremnum aureum

Water: once per week

Light: shade, indirect sunlight
